# TDA00 Character Notes

Auxiliary only. JP wording remains primary.

## ウィル / 威尔

- Full name appears as `ウィルバート・D・コリンズ`.
- Narrator/protagonist viewpoint for many non-speaker lines.
- A U.S. Marine tactical-machine pilot attached to the Black Knives.
- Often self-critical, restrained, and practical.
- In narration, he uses casual male first-person tone; in formal military speech, he switches to disciplined replies.
- Tends to worry about unit cohesion, Lilia's emotional state, and operational realism.

## リリア / 莉莉娅

- Full name appears as `リリア・シェルベリ`.
- U.S. Marine officer/survivor recovered from a lifeboat.
- Direct, intense, duty-driven, and emotionally shaken by survivor guilt.
- Uses formal military responses in front of superiors, but can be sharp and personal with Will.
- Her search urgency must not be softened into generic sadness; she is driven by duty to comrades.

## ダリル / 达里尔

- Full name appears as `ダリル・マクマナス`; nickname/style includes `Big Mom`.
- Black Knives leader and Marine lieutenant.
- Rough, maternal, blunt, profane, and morale-focused.
- Speech often uses casual feminine/rough markers such as `アタシ`, `～だよ`, `～しな`.
- She may use sexual/profane jokes in battle briefings. Do not over-polish unless user decides.

## メルヴィナ / 梅尔维娜

- Technical/engineering figure aboard JFK/base.
- Bright, teasing, fast-talking, and practical.
- Often uses humor to make harsh realities easier to face.
- Can sound playful, but her technical explanations should stay clear.

## オールストン准将 / 奥尔斯顿准将

- Senior commander aboard JFK.
- Formal, ceremonial, strategic, and political.
- Speeches should be dignified and military, not colloquial.

## 神宮司少佐 / 神宫司少佐

- Appears in Day 100 with Lilia.
- Stern, direct, and mission-focused.
- Lines carry authority and survival doctrine.

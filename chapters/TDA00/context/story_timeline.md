# TDA00 Story Timeline

This is an auxiliary timeline for translation review. It must not override JP wording.

## Prologue

- The migration fleet command ship `マルドゥーク` begins GS-engine operation and enters gravity-gradient navigation.
- A message prays for humanity's victory and Earth's restoration.
- Taylor launches `バビロン作戦`.

## Day 91

- Will and Daryl fly as Black Knives over the changed oceanic world after the Great Collapse.
- They conduct surveying, GPS checks, and relay-related work while operating from JFK.
- They find Lilia Shelbury in a lifeboat and recover her.
- Search for other survivors is impossible because JFK and its forces are themselves isolated, short on resources, and dependent on relay coverage.

## Day 93

- Will escorts Lilia after her recovery.
- Daryl and Allston explain the situation aboard JFK and the Marine detachment's status.
- The Black Knives/318th tactical unit has lost most of its members; only Daryl and Will remain from the original tactical-machine platoon.
- Lilia is assigned to operate an F-18E and join the detachment.

## Day 94

- Lilia adjusts poorly to JFK's environment and clashes with Will over search duties and command realities.
- Melvina appears as an engineer/technical officer who understands the carrier's practical limitations.
- Will explains that some mission information is protected by security clearance and `Need to know`.
- Conflict builds around survivors, secrecy, duty, and whether further search is possible.

## Day 95

- Will and Lilia remain tense after the previous clash.
- Daryl presses them back into functioning as a unit.
- Search and relay missions continue; communication limitations remain central.
- Lilia's survivor guilt and urgency to find others continue to strain her relationship with Will.

## Day 96

- Will and Lilia interact more directly while moving through JFK and the base.
- Melvina explains and contextualizes various ship/base systems and living conditions.
- The story turns toward memorialization and grief for the dead.
- Allston announces a unit-level funeral for those lost in Blue Ocean and related events.

## Day 97

- Will, Lilia, and Melvina prepare for the memorial.
- The flag and ceremonial materials become emotionally important despite their practical origin.
- Lilia begins to understand how meaning is made by survivors rather than supplied by objects alone.

## Day 98

- Allston delivers a memorial address.
- The speech frames the victory over BETA and the destruction of the Hives against the catastrophic cost of the Great Collapse.
- The funeral gives the survivors a shared way to carry the dead forward.

## Day 99

- The morning after the memorial begins with a subtle emotional shift.
- JFK faces a large BETA attack.
- The Black Knives coordinate with Navy tactical-machine units, ship guns, CIWS, and relay/communication systems.
- Battle language is sharp, compressed, profane, and command-oriented.
- Lilia plays a major role in the defense but is ultimately separated/lost in action.
- Will and Daryl continue fighting while carrying Lilia's name and the names of the dead.

## Day 100

- Lilia survives and is recovered by another force.
- Jinguuji Major speaks to Lilia.
- Lilia resolves to pass down the names and continue fighting in the name of the United States Marine Corps and her comrades.
- The chapter ends by reframing survival as a continuing mission on a transformed planet.
